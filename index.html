Talking Stick

Talking Stick App:

The app moderates conversation between two users.
Fill in the names of the users, and choose how long the session should be in minutes.
Choose the mode:
(locked to cut the time in half for the two users. Unlocked to let it go and then give you a self evaliation),
then hit on Start !
